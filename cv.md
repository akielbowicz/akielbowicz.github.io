## Augusto Kielbowicz (he/him/él)


Soy desarrollador de software financiero con experiencia en Python y Diseño Orientado a Objetos.
Además, estoy involucrado en programas de divulgación científica y en el uso de nuevas tecnologías en la investigación. Me apasionan y estoy involucrado en el desarrollo de software numérico para investigación científica, aplicaciones interactivas para la educación, análisis y visualización de datos basadas en Jupyter.
Mi objetivo es profundizar mi conocimiento y trasladar las buenas prácticas del desarrollo Open Source y Empresarial a la investigación académica.
Soy un asiduo participante y expositor en meetups y conferencias de tecnología.


I like to solve problems,

I grew up playing RPGs like Pokemon and Golden Sun, and there I learned that to solve harder problems one can gain experience by solving simpler problems. Also some platformers like Megaman X, where I learned that there are some skills that make some challenges much easier.

I enjoy stocasticty, thinking in possibilites and their range of consecuences that might follow from them.

I have a *Licenciatura* in Physiscs where I learned that to model nature one needs to search for simpler solutions (not easier), to try to find the right level of abstractions. Also, that there is always a path of least action.

I have been working as a Quantitative Developer and Software Engineer with a focus on numerical code for Valuation and Risk in Multi Asset Class systems. Most of the production code that I've developed is in Python or C#, for Research and EDA I prefer Julia, for tooling and scripting Python or (Powershell in Windows environmets).

I been exploring funcional languages: I've developed a few POCs in F# and Clojure, and this year I participate in a reading group that uses OCaml to reimplement [Computational Category Theory]()
